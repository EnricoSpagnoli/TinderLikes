How to get your X-Auth-Token:

The X-Auth-Token does not provide access to your account and we cannot read messages, swipe, change settings or unmatch for you. We only use the token to access your available previews

1. Go on Tinder and log-in to your account

2. Press Ctrl + Shift + I to open the DevTools

3. Press Ctrl + Shift + P and type "Network" in the search bar that pops up, then press Enter

4. Once on the "Network" tab, press Ctrl + R to refresh the page

5. After the page is done reloading, locate the "filter" field (usually at the top-left corner of the DevTools screen) and type "profile?"

6. Select any of the fields under the "Name" section to focus the request

7. In the section on the right, find and click on the "Headers" tab

8. Once in the headers, scroll all the way down and to see "x-auth-token". Copy the value next to it and paste it back here

Done!

Your X-Auth-Token may expire after a day or two. It is a session token meant to keep track of users on Tinder's servers
